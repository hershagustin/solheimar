The Path of Fire

A journey through the land's fiery soul, perfect for the bold and curious.

Experience the wild, volcanic heart of Iceland with our summertime tour, The Path of Fire. Bask in the midnight sun as you hike lava fields, explore active geothermal zones, and swim in naturally heated rivers.

Itinerary

Summer Tour: The Path of Fire

7 days, 10 destinations

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome to Reykjavík

Touch down at Keflavík Airport and head to Reykjavík for orientation and a welcome dinner. Meet your guide and fellow travellers over Icelandic comfort food.

Day 2: Golden Circle & Geothermal Wonders

Explore Þingvellir National Park, then continue to the erupting geysers at Haukadalur and the dramatic Gullfoss waterfall. End the day at Friðheimar greenhouse with a tomato soup lunch and a look at geothermal agriculture.

Day 3: Lava Hikes & Hot Springs

Hike the steaming trails of Reykjadalur Valley and soak in its natural hot rivers. In the afternoon, head to the Lava Centre for an interactive look at Iceland’s fiery tectonic power.

Day 4: West Iceland - Volcanic Coasts

Drive north to Akranes for a climb up the iconic lighthouse. Visit the powerful Deildartunguhver hot spring and the colorful village of Borgarnes for lunch. Option to add a geothermal spa stop in Krauma.

Day 5: Snæfellsnes Peninsula Cruise

Cruise through Breiðafjörður Bay to spot seabirds and sea stacks. After, explore the port town of Stykkishólmur and capture golden-hour photos of Mount Kirkjufell.

Day 6: South Coast & Black Sand Beaches

Visit Seljalandsfoss and Skógafoss waterfalls before walking the eerie black sands of Reynisfjara near Vík. Don’t forget your camera—this coastline is unforgettable.

Day 7: Reykjavík Farewell & Optional Sky Lagoon

Wrap up your trip with a final morning in Reykjavík for shopping or relaxing. Unwind at Sky Lagoon before heading to the airport—or extending your stay in the city.
